About the Workshop

The ImagEAT Workshop explores how emerging technologies — including advanced imaging, artificial intelligence (AI), virtual and augmented reality (VR/AR), and neuroscience — are transforming food safety, traceability, and consumer insights. It is supported by the METROFOOD-IT initiative.

Call for Papers

We invite high-quality submissions on food image analysis, AI in food science, AR for consumer engagement, and neuroscience in food decision-making. Accepted papers will be published in Springer LNCS.

Topics of Interest

  • AI-based food quality and safety assessment
  • Hyperspectral, CT, MSI, NMR, X-ray imaging for food
  • Image segmentation and classification
  • AR applications in food traceability and education
  • EEG and fMRI in consumer behavior analysis
